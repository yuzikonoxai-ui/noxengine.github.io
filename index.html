About NOX Engine

NOX Engine was built by Yuziko — a solo indie developer with a lifelong dream of creating a game engine. Built on the open-source Godot foundation and deeply customized with a unique identity.

This is version 1.0 — the first step. The engine is free, open-source, and will keep growing. If you make something with it, share it.
